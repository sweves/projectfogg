TAKE ME ON AN ADVENTURE

Project Fogg is a project ideated and developed as a final project for The Forge program at AKQA in San Francisco by Sarah Wever. Inspired by the thoughts and journeys of Flat Stanley and Karlie Kloss, Project Fogg aims to make use of 3D printing technology to allow any one to go on a spontaneous journey around the world (in 80 days perhaps?) in a parallel universe to your daily life. To view the stories being generated so far, be sure to check out all of our current active stories. To contribute, be sure to keep your eye out for one of our jars as you never know when you'll have the chance to make an impact on an ongoing epic journey!
